Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 April 2026

1. Introduction

Plot Twist is a conceptual demo platform for sharing plants between neighbors. We do not operate as a company and do not process real transactions.

2. Data we collect

  • Account information (if provided)
  • Content you post (images, text, listings)
  • Basic technical data (device/browser info)

3. How we use data

  • To display content on the platform
  • To run the demo functionality
  • To improve user experience

We do not use data for advertising or sales.

4. Sharing data

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

5. Data storage

Data is stored temporarily and may be deleted at any time.

6. Security

This is a demo system. No strong security guarantees are provided. Do not submit sensitive information.

7. Your choices

8. Changes

This policy may change as the project evolves.
